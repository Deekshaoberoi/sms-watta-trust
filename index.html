About Our Trust

SMS Watta Jivan Jagriti Trust is a family-funded charitable organization working in Ambala City for the welfare of society.

The Trust operates an Acupressure, Physiotherapy and Naturopathy Centre, conducts tree plantation drives, Langar Seva, health awareness programs, and humanitarian activities.
